ShopEasy Customer Support Policies

ORDER STATUS
Orders are tracked by order ID. Once an order is placed it goes through three stages: Processing, Shipped, and Delivered. Customers can check their order status at any time by providing their order ID to the support agent.

SHIPPING OPTIONS
ShopEasy offers three shipping methods:
- Standard shipping takes 3 to 5 business days and is free on orders over $50.
- Express shipping takes 1 to 2 business days and costs $12.99.
- Overnight shipping delivers the next business day and costs $24.99.
Once an order has been shipped, the shipping method cannot be changed.

ELECTRONICS RETURN POLICY
Electronics can be returned within 15 days of purchase. The item must be unopened and in its original packaging. Returns outside this window are not accepted.

CLOTHING RETURN POLICY
Clothing items can be returned within 30 days of purchase. All original tags must still be attached. Items that have been worn or washed are not eligible for return.

FOOD RETURN POLICY
Food items are non-returnable for safety and hygiene reasons. No exceptions are made regardless of the condition of the item.

FURNITURE RETURN POLICY
Furniture can be returned within 30 days of purchase provided it has not been assembled. Once assembled, furniture cannot be returned.

REFUNDS
Approved returns are refunded to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days. Store credit is available as an alternative and is processed within 1 business day.

DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS
If an item arrives damaged or defective, customers should contact support within 48 hours of delivery. ShopEasy will arrange a free return and send a replacement or issue a full refund.
